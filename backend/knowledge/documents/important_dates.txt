REGISTRATION PERIODS
---------------------
Priority registration opens approximately 3 weeks before the start of each quarter:
- Senior students (135+ credits): Register first.
- Junior students (90–134 credits): Register on day 2.
- Sophomore students (45–89 credits): Register on day 3.
- Freshman students (0–44 credits): Register on day 4.
- Open registration (all students): Begins day 5 and continues through the add period.

Registration dates for upcoming quarters:
- Spring 2026 registration: Opens February 23, 2026
- Summer 2026 registration: Opens April 27, 2026
- Autumn 2026 registration: Opens May 18, 2026

FINANCIAL AID DEADLINES
-------------------------
- FAFSA priority deadline: January 15 each year.
- UW General Scholarship Application: November 1 – February 15.
- Departmental scholarship deadlines: Vary; most are February 1.
- SHIP insurance waiver deadline: September 15 (Autumn), January 5 (Winter/Spring entry).
- Tuition payment due: 3rd Friday of each quarter.

GRADUATION APPLICATION DEADLINES
----------------------------------
Students planning to graduate must apply through MyUW:
- Autumn graduation: Apply by October 15, 2025.
- Winter graduation: Apply by January 15, 2026.
- Spring graduation: Apply by April 15, 2026.
- Summer graduation: Apply by July 15, 2026.

Late applications are accepted with a $25 late fee until 2 weeks before commencement.
